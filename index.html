This tool simulates a battle between your pokémon and every member of the Elite Four. It provides a full list of attacks that will one hit KO each member of the elite four, and lists the first level that the attack is guarenteed to KO. You can run the simulation multiple times without refreshing - this allows you to see and fill in the gaps in your team.
